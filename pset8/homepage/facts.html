Duck Facts

The rubber duck is CS50's mascot and a nod to "rubber duck debugging" — a technique where you debug by explaining your code aloud to an inanimate duck.

Articulating a problem step by step forces you to slow down and inspect assumptions, which is often enough to surface the mistake without any other help.

CS50 ships an in-browser rubber-duck AI assistant, keeping the mascot theme alive while helping students reason about their code.
